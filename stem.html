ABOUT STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) is among the strands of study in senior high school that is taught to students with a inclination towards scientific and technical subjects. STEM aims to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills necessary for engineering, medicine, computer science, and other scientific subjects. Students under the STEM strand undergo hands-on experiments, research studies, and mathematical calculations to enhance their knowledge of the natural world and technological innovations. This strand is best suited for students who want to become engineers, doctors, architects, programmers, or any other career that needs a solid science and mathematics background.
