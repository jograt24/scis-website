Education serves as the foundation of a bright future, and Sto. Cristo Integrated School, located in Barangay Santo Cristo, Tarlac City, stands as a beacon of learning and growth. As a public institution dedicated to academic excellence, the school provides comprehensive education from elementary to senior high school, ensuring that students receive the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in their chosen careers.

One of the highlights of Sto. Cristo Integrated School is its Senior High School (SHS) program, which offers a variety of strands under the K to 12 curriculum. These strands allow students to focus on fields that align with their interests and future aspirations, preparing them for both higher education and employment opportunities.

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand is ideal for students who are passionate about scientific discoveries, problem-solving, and innovation. It focuses on developing critical thinking and analytical skills, making it the perfect choice for those who aspire to become engineers, doctors, IT specialists, or scientists.
